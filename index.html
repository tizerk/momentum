Hello, We Are Momentum.

Designed by UC Santa Barbara students, our app helps people with busy schedules stay consistent with their fitness goals. We believe fitness isn't about perfection, but about showing up, building sustainable habits, and giving grace along the way. We wish to reinforce the idea that breaks do not equal failure. Life gets in the way, and that's okay. What matters most is long-term progress, not short-term perfection. This app was created as part of CMPSC 185: Human-Computer Interaction.

Our Design Process

Here are the key steps behind our final product.

Needfinding

GR1

Slides (pdf) Slides (pptx)

We conducted interviews and needfinding on a group of participants regarding the domain of goals. For each participant, we analyzed their insights and needs and was able to gather 4 key gatherings leading into the next GR.

POVs, HMWs & Experience Prototypes

GR2

Slides (pdf) Slides (pptx)

We narrowed our domain to gym users and conducted an additional 2 interviews. We then created 3 POVs and 30+ HMWs based on the insights we gathered, and created experience prototypes to test them out.
